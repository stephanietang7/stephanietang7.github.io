Falling Flower is a contemporary restaurant located in the heart of Los Angeles that focuses on floral-infused foods, drinks and desserts. Inspired by many flavors from different cultures, Falling Flower changes its entree dishes seasonally to allow old and new diners to experience unique flavors. Falling Flower redefines traditional dishes and infuses them with a touch of modern taste using organically and locally sourced meats, vegetables, fruits and grains.
